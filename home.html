About us

What we are?

Welcome to Page and Pillow, founded in 2025 by Santosh and our passionate team at The Minimalist Readers. Based in Gujarat, India, we believe books are the best replacement for endless scrolling on social media apps. Our mission is simple: to inspire mindful reading and bring depth back into everyday life. With a minimalist approach we curate each title intentionally — every book must offer knowledge, practical experience, or both. We believe knowledge needs learning and experience needs practice — books are the companions that provide both.

Our Mission

At Page and Pillow, our mission is to create a space where readers can discover carefully chosen books without distractions. In a world overflowing with noise and endless scrolling, we believe in a minimal but powerful design — one that puts books at the center. Every feature on our website is crafted to make exploration simple, meaningful, and enjoyable. Here, users will find more than books — they will find knowledge, inspiration, and the quiet joy of mindful reading.
